Automaton Toolkit

A web-based tool for testing, minimizing, generating NFA from regular expressions and comparing two DFA.

🧪

DFA String Testing

Define your DFA and test input strings to see if they are accepted or rejected.

 🔄

Regex to NFA

Convert regular expressions into Non-deterministic Finite Automata and test strings against them.

 ⚡

DFA Minimization

Minimize your DFA to reduce the number of states.

 🔍

DFA Equivalence

Compare two DFAs to determine if they are equivalent.
